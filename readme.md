# Exploring Virtual Reality

**[School of Computing](https://computing.unl.edu/)**  
**[Johnny Carson Center for Emerging Media Arts](https://arts.unl.edu/carson-center/)**  
**[University of Nebraska-Lincoln](https://unl.edu)**  

EMAR 440/CSCE 352 is a collaboration between Steven Kolbe, Associate Professor
in the Johnny Carson School of Theatre & Film and Dr. Chris Bourke, Professor
of Practice in the School of Computing that is now celebrating its 10th
anniversary (2017-2026).  This *sandbox* course teams new media students with
computing students to collaboratively create innovative VR games, experiences
and other projects.
